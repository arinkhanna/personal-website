My Work

Spending my summers in India, I began to notice a common issue, a lack of clean drinking water in rural villages. I decided to do something about it and co-founded this nonprofit. After over 7 years we have raised over $25,000 and provided our services in over 6 villages. We also have created over 20 jobs for women, empowering them in normally patriarchal communities.
